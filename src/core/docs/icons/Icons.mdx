# Icons

Our iconography is designed around [Lucide icons](https://lucide.dev/), a set of open-source icons that are simple, clean, and easy to understand.

<Canvas of={IconStories.Default} />

## Why Lucide?

- **Simplicity:** Lucide icons are crafted with a focus on simplicity, ensuring they remain clear and legible even at smaller sizes.
- **Consistency:** The uniform style of Lucide icons promotes a consistent visual language across our applications.
- **Open Source:** Being open source, Lucide offers flexibility and community-driven improvements.
